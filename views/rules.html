The Rules

In short

We're playing 1v1 to see once and for all who has the biggest dick. It's a league format with everyone scheduled to play each other home and away. The schedule will be published around the 15th of October and everyone will have 2 games to complete per week until the league finishes. Winner takes nothing.

The game

  • Type: Rumble 1v1
  • Score limit: 25
  • Time limit: 10 minutes
  • Map: Any
  • Supers: Allowed
  • Heavy: Not allowed

What the fuck is this home and away nonsense? Easy. Home player chooses the map for "home advantage". Got a favourite or know your opponent is shit on one? Those would be good choices. The home player is on the left of the fixture list.

You've banned heavy? Why you do dis? Because a block of heavy and a machine gun will get you 5 kills in the time it takes for the ammo to respawn. Not fun.

But supers are OK? Yep. It's a fairly level playing field and realistically you'll only get 1 kill.

Scoring

  • Win: 3 points
  • Draw: 1 point
  • Loss: 0 points

The winner sends a screenshot of the final result to LordOrchard and he will update the results table accordingly.

Most points at the end of the league wins. If point scores are level then best kill differencs (kills - deaths) wins, then it's most kills. Christ knows what we'll do if we're still tied then. Probably a brawl.

Schedule

UPDATED 18/11/19: Games will take place weekly between Destiny resets, currently Tuesdays at 5pm (GMT). You will have 2 games per week. 1 home and 1 away against different opponents. By the end of the league everyone will have played everyone twice.

Attendance is key for the league to work and for everyone to have a good time. You only have to turn up for 2 games per week. That's 20 minutes. You can do it.

You can view your fixtures for the entire league season as soon as they are published. Each week you and your opponent are responsible for arranging your match.

  • If you can not do a particular week you forfeit the matches and your opponents get a no-score win
  • If you do not turn up to an agreed match you forfeit the match and your opponent gets a no-score win
  • If you both can not agree on a time that's also a no-score forfeit draw
  • To claim a forfeit win you only have to demonstrate that you tried to, or managed to, arrange a scheduled match and your opponent didn't respond or didn't turn up

These rules are deliberately harsh to keep the games going. We want games, not excuses.

Prizes

There are no prizes.

  • 1st Place - nothing
  • 2nd Place - still nothing
  • 3rd Place - fuck all

Last place gets you a 2am, in person, double-dip teabagging experience from the one-and-only Lord 'scrote-dangler' Orchard on a night that is really inconvenient for you. If that doesn't sound like your cup of tea, don't lose. Failing that sleep with one eye open and your mouth closed.
